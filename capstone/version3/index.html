It began as a university farm in 1905. Then in 1908, three years later,
it opened its doors as the University Farm School.


The school’s main focus surrounded education, research, and agriculture.
As the school grew, overtime, they began to expand their programs to include
more than agriculture, and are now known as a renowned research university that
prioritizes public service. Today, UC Davis is known for its excellence in
teaching, research, and outreach across a wide range of disciplines.
